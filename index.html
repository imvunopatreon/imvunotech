Life in the Netherlands

I am based in a lovely and welcoming North Brabant city of Eindhoven, in the Netherlands, since 2023. I really did not know what to expect from the Netherlands communities as I arrived out of a necessity to make a living. I have an interest in acquiring an Estonian e-residency because my ideas span using computing power running on hosted cloud services, third party or custom. Estonia has the most favourable operational conditions for online businesses in the EU from regulation, taxation and other points of view. I don't have to live there to make this happen (hence, e-residency). The Netherlands is central enough for me for other EU spaces to reach, plus it is quiet friendly in many aspects, as an African. My angle is to also attach a tourism aspect to this, definitely something to look forward to. I intend to share more on my interests in the up-coming Github based projects.
